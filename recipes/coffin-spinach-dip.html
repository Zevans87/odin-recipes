Coffin Spinach Dip Recipe

Description

Every party needs a good dip and for your Halloween party no dip can beat a nice spinach dip stuffed in a bread bowl made to resemble a coffin!
This spooky addition to your party will be gone before you know it. So you might want to make 2!

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chopped Spinach
  • Vegetable Seasoning Pack
  • Sour Cream
  • Mayonnaise
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chopped Water Chestnuts
  • Dippers - Blue corn tortilla chips, pretzel chips, crackers, cut Vegetables, or bread cubes.
  • Firm Loaf of Unsliced Bread.

Steps

  1. Cut loaf of bread in half, horizontally, while leaving a hinge.
  2. Hollow out the bottom portion of the loaf of bread making it look like an empty coffin.
  3. Break up spinach into smaller pieced to make it easier to combine with the other Ingredients.
  4. In a bowl mix all ingredients together.
  5. Cover the bowl and let the dip sit for at least 2 hours.
  6. Fill coffin bread bowl with spinach dip, place on an oblong plate, surround with chosen dippers, and enjoy!
